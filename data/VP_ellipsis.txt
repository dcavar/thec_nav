Tł'áá hwiił ___ dashicheii hónááná
----
Tł'áá hwiił tł'áá dashicheii hónááná
# Khai Willard: https://talkingdictionary.swarthmore.edu/navajo_verb_mode/?fields=all&semantic_ids=&q=*car&highlight%5B%5D=0
# Translation eng: The child is visiting the grandfather and the grandmother.

Ahóó asdzáán tł'áá ___ hwiił hónááná.
----
Ahóó asdzáán tł'áá ahóó hwiił hónááná.
# Khai Willard
# Translation eng: I'm going to see the child and the grandmother.
