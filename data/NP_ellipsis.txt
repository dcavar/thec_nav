Ch’óóshdą́ą́dą́ą́’ shichidí naaki. K’ad ’éí ___ t’ááłá’i.́
----
Ch’óóshdą́ą́dą́ą́’ shichidí naaki. K’ad ’éí shichidí t’ááłá’i
# added by Khai Willard
# https://talkingdictionary.swarthmore.edu/navajo_verb_mode/?fields=all&semantic_ids=&q=*&highlight%5B%5D=0
# Translation eng: I used to have two cars, now I only have one.

Tł'áá hwiił ___ hashtł'ish.
----
Tł'áá hwiił tł'áá hashtł'ish.
# added by Khai Willard
# https://talkingdictionary.swarthmore.edu/navajo_verb_mode/?fields=all&semantic_ids=&q=*&highlight%5B%5D=0
# Translation eng: The child is running and barking.

Díí chidí, háísh ___ át’é?
----
Díí chidí, háísh bichidí át’é?
# added by Khai Willard
# Navajo Made Easier, Irvy W. Goossen
Translation eng: This car, whose is it?
