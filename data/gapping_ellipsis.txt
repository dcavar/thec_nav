Bill naané jooł daakalígíí, dóó Anna ___ jooł nikídílniihí.
----
Bill naané jooł daakalígíí, dóó Anna naané jooł nikídílniihí.
# added by Khai Willard
# The Navajo Verb: A Grammar for Students and Scholars, Leonard M. Faltz
# Translation eng: Bill is playing baseball, and Anna basketball.

----
Sally bilasáana ła'yiya, dóó John didzétsoh ła'yiya.
# added by Khai Willard
# The Navajo Verb: A Grammar for Students and Scholars, Leonard M. Faltz
# Translation eng: Sally is eating an apple, and John a peach.

# added by Khai Willard
# Bogal-Allbritten, Elizabeth A., "Building Meaning in Navajo" (2016). Doctoral Dissertations. 552.
